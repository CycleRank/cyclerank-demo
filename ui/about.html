Authors

Luca Cavalcanti

Luca Cavalcanti is an undergraduate student at the University of Trento, Italy and is currently working on his thesis at Eurecat - Centre Tecnològic de Catalunya in Barcelona, delving in the world of relevance algorithms on graphs and their visualization.

Cristian Consonni

Cristian Consonni is an Advanced Research Scientist in the Data Science and Big Data Analytics unit at Eurecat - Centre Tecnològic de Catalunya in Barcelona. He works on Computational Social Science, studying social phenomena through digital platforms and, in particular, the dynamics of online communities. He received his Ph.D. in computer science from the University of Trento, Italy, with a thesis on extracting knowledge from hyperlinks between Wikipedia pages.

Martin Brugnara

Martin Brugnara is a Senior Member of Technical Staff at Oracle Labs Zurich, where he works on Graphs and RDBMS. He received a Ph.D. in computer science from the University of Trento with a thesis on Understanding and Managing Complex Datasets.

David Laniado

David Laniado is Senior Research Scientist in the Data Science and Big Data Analytics unit at Eurecat. He obtained from Politecnico di Milano his Ph.D. in Information Engineering in 2012. His research focuses on Computational Social Science, and in particular in the study of online interactions and online communities. He is co-author of more than 50 academic publications in this field.

Alberto Monrresor

Alberto Montresor is a Full Professor at the University of Trento, Italy. Alberto Montresor received his Ph.D. in Computer Science from the University of Bologna, Italy in 2000. He joined the University of Trento in 2005 and became a full Professor in 2020. Since 2022, he has been a member of the Academic Senate and serves as the Delegate of the Rector for Third Mission at the University of Trento. Montresor has published over 120 papers in the field of distributed systems, with a focus on large-scale decentralized systems. He was recognized as a Highly Cited Researcher in both 2020 and 2022 by Stanford University's Highly Cited Researchers Ranking.
